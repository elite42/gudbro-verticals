## 1. Executive Summary

GUDBRO is a multi-vertical PWA platform empowering small businesses in Southeast Asia's tourism and hospitality sector. The website serves as the primary marketing and onboarding channel for business owners across 6 verticals.

### Core Value Proposition
**"Zero commission. Full control. Beautiful digital presence for your business."**

---

## 2. Business Context

### Target Markets
- **Primary:** Vietnam (Da Nang, Hoi An, Ho Chi Minh, Hanoi)
- **Secondary:** Thailand, Indonesia (Bali), Philippines
- **Tertiary:** Broader Southeast Asia expansion

### Target Customers (B2B)

| Vertical | Customer Profile | Pain Points |
|----------|------------------|-------------|
| **Coffeeshop** | Cafe/restaurant owners, 25-50 y/o | Paper menus, language barriers, no digital presence |
| **Tours** | Street vendors, small tour operators | Cardboard signs, can't explain tours, lose sales to big apps |
| **Stays** | Guesthouse/villa owners, 1-5 properties | 15-25% OTA commissions, can't upsell services |
| **Wellness** | Spa/massage shop owners | No booking system, WhatsApp chaos |
| **Rentals** | Motorbike/car rental shops | Paper contracts, payment issues |
| **Backoffice** | All above (management dashboard) | Need simple admin tools |

## 5. GUDBRO Partnership Network

**Key Differentiator** - Cross-merchant ecosystem

### How It Works

```
┌─────────────┐     ┌─────────────┐     ┌─────────────┐
│  Coffeeshop │◄───►│    Tours    │◄───►│    Stays    │
└─────────────┘     └─────────────┘     └─────────────┘
       ▲                   ▲                   ▲
       └───────────────────┴───────────────────┘
                    GUDBRO Customer
```

### Value Exchange

| Party | Benefit |
|-------|---------|
| **Customer** | Exclusive discounts at partner businesses |
| **Referrer** | Commission on referred sales (optional) |
| **Provider** | Qualified leads from trusted sources |
| **GUDBRO** | Sticky ecosystem, reduced churn |

### GUDBRO Pass
- Digital pass for customers
- QR code + alphanumeric code
- Identify as GUDBRO customer at any partner
- Unlock exclusive deals

### Business Model Impact
> "Refer 3-4 customers/month → subscription pays for itself"

## 8. Design Direction

### Inspiration
**meandu.com** - Clean, professional, trustworthy

### Key Characteristics
- **Clean layouts** with generous whitespace
- **Professional blue** as primary accent
- **Modern typography** - distinctive but readable
- **High-quality photography** - real businesses, real people
- **Mobile-first** - most visitors on phones
- **Fast loading** - target markets have variable connectivity

### Color Palette (Suggested)

```css
--primary: #2563EB;      /* Blue - trust, professionalism */
--primary-dark: #1E40AF;
--secondary: #10B981;    /* Green - growth, success */
--accent: #F59E0B;       /* Amber - warmth, hospitality */
--neutral-50: #F9FAFB;
--neutral-900: #111827;
```

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, distinctive display font
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif, excellent readability
- **Avoid:** Inter, Roboto, Arial (too generic)

### Visual Elements
- Subtle gradients (not purple-on-white cliche)
- Soft shadows for depth
- Rounded corners (friendly, approachable)
- Icons: consistent outline style
- Photography: warm, authentic, Southeast Asian context
